Use 2 peripherals, the keyboard and 7-segment LED, to build a simple pocket computer. Support the
+, -, *, /, %. Because the keyboard does not have the above keys, we will use the keys
o Press key a to enter the + calculation
o Press b key to enter calculation –
o Press key c to enter calculation *
o Press d key to enter calculation /
o Press the e key to enter the % calculation
o Press the f key to enter =
Specific requirements are as follows:
o When pressing the number keys, the LED is displayed. Because there are only 2 LEDs, only the last 2 numbers are displayed. Wallet
For example, when pressing key 1 → displaying 01. When further pressing key 2 → displaying 12. When further pressing
key 3 → display 23.
o After entering the number, the calculation + - * /% will be entered
o After pressing the f key (= sign), calculate and display the results on LED.
